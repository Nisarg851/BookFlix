About me

I'm an avid reader with an insatiable curiosity. My favorite pastime is getting lost in the pages of a captivating book, where I can explore new worlds, meet fascinating characters, and learn about different cultures and ideas. I love books that challenge me intellectually and emotionally, and I'm always looking for new recommendations. Whether I'm curled up on the couch with a classic novel or listening to an audiobook during my commute, reading is an essential part of my life.

The Da Vinci Code is a thrilling mystery novel by Dan Brown that follows symbologist Robert Langdon as he investigates a murder in the Louvre Museum. In Yoon Ha Lee's Moonstorm, Hwa Young, an orphan from a destroyed rebel moon, dreams of becoming an elite lancer pilot for the empire that took her family.
